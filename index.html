How this course works

Each week follows a loose arc: we introduce new concepts in lecture, build on them with in-class demos and exercises, and then apply them in the Monday afternoon lab. The schedule below shows the general shape of a typical week — but, especially in the first few weeks, we'll feel this out together as a class.
