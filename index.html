How this course works

Each week follows a loose arc: we introduce new concepts in lecture, build on them with in-class demos and exercises, and then apply them in the Monday afternoon lab. Especially in the first few weeks, we'll feel this out together as a class.

First time here? Make sure you have a CSDMS ExploreHub account before clicking any buttons. Contact Dr. Morey if you need access. You will also need a free GitHub account — set one up with your Vanderbilt email before Week 6. When you open a notebook, rename it immediately by adding your last name (e.g. 00_course_intro_Smith.ipynb) before you start working.
